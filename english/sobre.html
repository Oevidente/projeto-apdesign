Who we are?

We are Ap Design, a team of designers specialized in social media.
In addition to social media, we work with visual identity, motion graphics and editorial design. We have been in the market since 2019, and we have already worked with clients from different follow-ups.
We work remotely and quickly to always bring the best service to our customers.
Our team is divided between tasks focused on printed arts and another for digital arts.

Our history

It all started when in 2010 André joined
SAGA (School of Arts Games and Animation)
and as soon as he started making his first image edits he decided to show it to Paulo,
his brother.
André then taught Paulo what he knew and so they both embarked on the path of Graphic Design.
Later, Paulo came to study at Unibratec studying Computer Graphics,
and André went to IFPE to take the same course.
already they had been planning to work together for a while and that was when the opportunity came up in 2019, Paulo invited André to work together, and that was when
Ap Design was born.
